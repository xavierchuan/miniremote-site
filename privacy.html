Privacy Policy

隐私政策

Last updated: 2026-05-27

Overview

Handpad turns your iPhone or iPad into a wireless trackpad and keyboard for your Mac. We respect your privacy and built the app on a single principle: your data stays on your devices.

Data Collection

Handpad does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No user accounts
  • No data sent to any server, ever

Local Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Device nickname — a name you choose, used to identify your devices over Bluetooth and WiFi
  • Custom shortcut layouts — button configurations you create
  • Encryption key — a randomly generated key stored in the iOS Keychain, used to secure the WiFi link to your Mac
  • App preferences — UI settings, last-used page, onboarding state

None of this data ever leaves your local network.

Network Communication

Handpad communicates exclusively with the paired Handpad Mac app, using:

  • Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) for pairing and low-bandwidth control
  • Local WiFi (TCP, same network only) for higher-bandwidth input streams

All WiFi traffic is end-to-end encrypted using AES-256-GCM with a key established during Bluetooth pairing. No internet connection is required or used.

Permissions

  • Bluetooth — required to discover and pair with your Mac
  • Local Network — used to send input to your Mac over WiFi for lower latency
  • Microphone / Speech Recognition — only used if you invoke iOS dictation from the system keyboard. Audio is processed by iOS on-device (or by Apple's servers, depending on your iOS settings), not by Handpad. Handpad only receives the recognized text.

Third-Party Services

Handpad does not integrate any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking. The app is fully self-contained.

Children's Privacy

Handpad does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

Data Retention & Deletion

Because we don't collect any data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete on our end. To remove all locally stored Handpad data, simply uninstall the app — iOS will clear its Keychain entries and stored preferences.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

概述

Handpad 把你的 iPhone 或 iPad 变成 Mac 的无线触控板和键盘。我们尊重你的隐私,并基于一个原则设计这款应用:你的数据只留在你的设备上。

数据收集

Handpad 不收集、存储或传输任何个人数据。具体来说:

  • 无分析或追踪
  • 无广告
  • 无用户账号
  • 不向任何服务器发送数据,永不

本地数据

App 仅在 你本机设备上 存储以下数据:

  • 设备昵称 — 你自己起的名字,用于在蓝牙和 WiFi 上识别你的设备
  • 自定义快捷键布局 — 你创建的按钮配置
  • 加密密钥 — 随机生成的密钥,存于 iOS Keychain,用于加密与 Mac 的 WiFi 链路
  • App 偏好设置 — 界面设置、上次使用的页面、引导状态

这些数据永不离开你的局域网。

网络通信

Handpad 只与配对的 Handpad Mac 端 app 通信,使用:

  • 蓝牙低功耗 (BLE) 用于配对和低带宽控制
  • 本地 WiFi (TCP,仅同一网络) 用于高带宽输入流

所有 WiFi 流量使用 AES-256-GCM 端到端加密,密钥在蓝牙配对阶段建立。无需也不会使用互联网连接。

权限

  • 蓝牙 — 用于发现并与你的 Mac 配对
  • 本地网络 — 用于在 WiFi 上向 Mac 发送输入,降低延迟
  • 麦克风 / 语音识别 — 仅当你从系统键盘调用 iOS 语音听写时使用。音频由 iOS 在本机处理(或根据你的 iOS 设置由 Apple 服务器处理),而非由 Handpad 处理。Handpad 只接收识别后的文本。

第三方服务

Handpad 未集成任何第三方 SDK 用于分析、广告、崩溃上报或追踪。App 完全自包含。

儿童隐私

Handpad 不从任何人收集数据,包括 13 岁以下儿童。App 对所有年龄段都安全。

数据保留与删除

由于我们不收集任何数据,我们这一端没有任何数据可保留或删除。如需删除本机所有 Handpad 数据,只需卸载 app — iOS 会清除其 Keychain 条目和存储的偏好设置。
