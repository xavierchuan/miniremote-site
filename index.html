Mini Remote - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-26

Overview

Mini Remote turns your iPhone or iPad into a wireless trackpad and keyboard for your Mac. We respect your privacy and designed the app with a simple principle: your data stays on your devices.

Data Collection

Mini Remote does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No user accounts
  • No data sent to any server

Local Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Device nickname — a name you choose, used for Bluetooth/WiFi identification between your devices
  • Custom keyboard layouts — button configurations you create
  • Encryption key — a randomly generated key stored in the iOS Keychain, used to secure WiFi communication with your Mac

None of this data ever leaves your local network.

Network Communication

Mini Remote communicates exclusively with the paired Mac app over Bluetooth Low Energy and/or local WiFi. All WiFi traffic is encrypted (AES-256-GCM). No internet connection is required or used.

Permissions

  • Bluetooth — required to connect to Mac
  • Local Network — used for low-latency WiFi connection on the same network
  • Microphone / Speech Recognition — only used if you invoke iOS dictation via the system keyboard; audio is processed by iOS, not by Mini Remote

Children's Privacy

Mini Remote does not collect any data from anyone, including children.
