About Handpad

Handpad turns your iPhone into a precision wireless trackpad and keyboard for your Mac. Slide, type, present, and control — Bluetooth pairs instantly, WiFi auto-boosts for low latency, and your data never leaves your local network.

关于 Handpad

Handpad 把 iPhone 变成 Mac 的精确无线触控板和键盘。滑动、打字、演示、控制 — 蓝牙瞬连,WiFi 自动加速降延迟,数据全在本地,从不离开你的局域网。

Getting Started

  • Install the free Handpad companion app on your Mac
  • Open both the iPhone app and the Mac app
  • Enable Bluetooth on both devices
  • First connection pairs over Bluetooth; subsequent sessions auto-boost to WiFi if available on the same local network

Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题

Does Handpad work without internet?

Yes. Handpad communicates entirely over Bluetooth and your local WiFi network. No internet connection is required or used.

Why does the iPhone app say "Waiting for Mac"?

The iPhone is ready but hasn't seen the Mac yet. Open the Handpad Mac app, make sure Bluetooth is enabled on both devices, and keep them within ~10 meters of each other.

Can one iPhone control multiple Macs?

You can pair with multiple Macs, but only one active connection at a time. Switch between paired Macs from the Mac picker in the iPhone app's settings.

Does Handpad support Chinese / Japanese / Korean input?

Yes — Handpad uses your iPhone's native keyboard, so every input method, dictionary, and custom shortcut you have on iOS works exactly the same when typing into your Mac.

Can I customize the shortcut buttons?

Yes. Tap "Edit" on the Shortcuts page to rearrange buttons, change actions, or create new layouts for different scenarios (movies, presentations, games, etc.).

Why does the WiFi connection feel laggy?

Make sure your iPhone and Mac are on the same WiFi network and, if possible, on a 5 GHz band. Mesh networks with band steering can sometimes route the two devices through different access points, increasing latency.

不联网能用 Handpad 吗?

可以。Handpad 仅在蓝牙和你的局域网之间通信,完全不需要也不会使用互联网。

iPhone 一直显示 "Waiting for Mac" 怎么办?

这表示 iPhone 端就绪了但还没看到 Mac。请打开 Mac 端的 Handpad,确认两台设备的蓝牙都开着,并且距离不超过 10 米。

一台 iPhone 能控制多台 Mac 吗?

可以配对多台 Mac,但同一时间只能激活一台连接。在 iPhone 端 app 的设置里可以切换 Mac。

支持中文 / 日文 / 韩文输入吗?

完全支持。Handpad 使用 iPhone 原生键盘,你在 iOS 上设置好的所有输入法、词库、自定义短语都能正常工作。

能自定义快捷键按钮吗?

可以。点击 Shortcuts 页面右上角的 "Edit",可以重排按钮、修改动作,或为不同场景(看片、演示、游戏)创建新的布局。

WiFi 模式下感觉延迟很大?

请确认 iPhone 和 Mac 在同一 WiFi 网络下,尽量使用 5 GHz 频段。Mesh 路由器的 band steering 偶尔会让两台设备走不同 AP,导致延迟升高。
